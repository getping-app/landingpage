About getping

We believe that meaningful relationships are the foundation of a fulfilling life. That's why we created getping - to help you nurture and maintain the connections that matter most.

Our Mission

In our increasingly digital world, it's easy to lose touch with the people who matter most. We're on a mission to change that by providing tools that make relationship management effortless and meaningful.

getping isn't just another CRM - it's your personal relationship assistant that helps you remember important details, stay in touch, and build deeper connections with the people in your life.

Our Story

getping was born from a simple realization: despite having hundreds of contacts in our phones, many of us struggle to maintain meaningful relationships. We found ourselves losing touch with old friends, forgetting important details about colleagues, and missing opportunities to connect.

Founded in 2025 by a team of relationship-focused technologists, getping started as a passion project to solve this universal problem. We wanted to create something that wasn't just about managing contacts, but about nurturing human connections.

Today, getping helps thousands of people maintain stronger relationships, remember important moments, and never let meaningful connections fade away. We're just getting started on our mission to help people build more fulfilling personal and professional networks.
